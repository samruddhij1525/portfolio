05 — User Persona

Three Archetypes. One Shared Frustration.

Priya, 21 — The Student

Problem

Can't access Zudio's latest collection because she lives far from the store and studies late at night.

Goals

Browse new arrivals, build wishlists, and order from hostel without wasting time traveling.

Frustrations

No stock visibility, forced login on other apps, and high prices on Myntra.

Rajeesh, 29 — The Professional

Problem

Only has weekends free but often finds items out of stock after traveling to the store.

Goals

Check real-time stock, fast checkout, and option for same-day pickup.

Frustrations

Wasted weekend trips, long checkout processes, and no way to reserve items.

Kavita, 36 — The Family Shopper

Problem

Shops for entire family but has no way to compare sizes and stock across family members.

Goals

Multi-person wishlist, family cart, and easy size comparison for kids & adults.

Frustrations

Complicated cart for multiple people, inconsistent sizing, and no family sharing option.
