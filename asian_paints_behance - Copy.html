01 — CONCEPT

What if choosing a
paint color felt like play?

The Asian Paints Interactive Experience Store turns color selection into a playful, sensory journey. Rather than browsing swatches under flat store lighting, visitors step into immersive rooms, paint freely on large walls, learn the psychology behind each hue, and capture their color moment — before ever picking up a brush at home.

“The store blends tech, interaction, and emotion — helping customers feel color before choosing it.”

04 — FINAL RENDERS

Final Rendered Design

An immersive 3D experience store designed to bring the world of color to life through surreal, larger-than-life installations. This spatial design utilizes oversized interactive elements from dripping architectural walls to surreal brush installations, to guide visitors through a tactile and high-energy exploration of color and creativity.
